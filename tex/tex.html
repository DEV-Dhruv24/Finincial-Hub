Tax Details

Indian Tax System

The Indian tax system is progressive, meaning that the more you earn, the higher the rate at which you are taxed. Below are the tax slabs:

  • Up to ₹2,50,000: No tax
  • ₹2,50,001 to ₹5,00,000: 5% of income exceeding ₹2,50,000
  • ₹5,00,001 to ₹10,00,000: 10% of income exceeding ₹5,00,000
  • ₹10,00,001 to ₹50,00,000: 20% of income exceeding ₹10,00,000
  • Above ₹50,00,000: 30% of income exceeding ₹50,00,000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How is the income tax calculated?

Income tax is calculated based on the applicable tax slabs. The income is divided into different slabs, and tax is applied at different rates for each slab.

Can I switch between currencies?

Yes, you can select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu, and the tax amount will be converted accordingly.
